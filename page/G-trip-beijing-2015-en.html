G-Trip Beijing April 27 - April 30

The second annual G-Trip Beijing, GWC’s signature executive trip for mobile leaders, takes place April 27 - April 30, 2015. G-Trip Beijing is a four-day executive business trip which objective is to connect international business executives with influential Chinese companies and its leaders to facilitate relationship building. Surging demand for smartphones in China has created a competitive field which makes it the most sought after mobile market in the East. Entering this complex market can be a daunting experience, as Chinese business culture is based on relationships and trust and requires active networking and personal introductions for successful partnerships. This program offers its participants workshops on how to do business effectively in China and strategies from experts on how to work with the world’s fastest growing economy. Participants will have access to exclusive networking events, business focused dinners, company visits to leading Chinese companies in Beijing, such as Baidu, Chukong, Tencent, UCWeb and Xiaomi. The program kicks-off with a business-etiquette crash course on the do's and don’ts of doing business in/with Asian culture to help maximize business deal making and networking at the GMIC Welcome Dinner exclusive to VIP attendees. In addition, participants will have access to the GMIC conference program consisting of more than 150 sessions addressing the Asian and international mobile Internet market.[/column][column type="one-third" last="true" class="pic-col"][image src="http://7u2l46.com5.z0.glb.qiniucdn.com/2015/g-trip/baidu-photo.jpg" type="thumbnail" style="width:300px"][image src="http://7u2l46.com5.z0.glb.qiniucdn.com/2015/g-trip/tencent-photo.jpg" type="thumbnail" style="width:300px"][/column]
