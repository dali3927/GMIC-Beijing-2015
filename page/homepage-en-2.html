Mobile Everything!

Mobile is extending far beyond the smartphone into all aspects of our lives. It continues to innovate and change forms from phones to tablets, clothing, cars, smart TVs to robots. Our devices are learning about us and talking amongst themselves to reshape the mobile industry. Join over 25,000 mobile industry executives, entrepreneurs, developers and investors from 60+ countries at the Global Mobile Internet Conference (GMIC) Beijing 2015.

China National Convention Center, Beijing | April 28-30, 2015

G20 Summit The G20 brings together key industry leaders to discuss mobile Internet opportunities within their respective markets, and globally. The G20 is an invite-only, closed-door, and off-the-record event.
Thought Leader Keynotes, fireside chats and panels from over 100 visionaries and executives representing the world’s leading mobile, internet, cloud and big data companies that are redefining the future of the industry and the world.
Global Game Summit GGS aims to connect, educate and promote game developers, while helping them to interact with game platforms, publishers, distributors, VCs and media from all over the world.
G-Startup G-Startup focused panels and keynotes from successful founders and investors plus 20 startups from around the world competing for prizes and a chance to pitch on the Main Stage.
